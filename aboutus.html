Welcome! At YAWYE

YAWYE is a versatile tool used by individuals for effective weight management, whether the goal is weight loss or weight gain. For weight loss, users can track their calorie intake and exercise to create a calorie deficit, aiding in achieving their desired weight loss goals. The app assists in setting a personalized daily calorie target based on current weight, activity level, and desired rate of weight loss. Conversely, individuals aiming to gain weight can use YAWYE to monitor their calorie intake, ensuring they consume a surplus of calories paired with adequate protein to support muscle growth. This comprehensive approach to calorie tracking and nutritional monitoring supports users in achieving their specific weight management objectives.
